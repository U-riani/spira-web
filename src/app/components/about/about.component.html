About us

Welcome to the world of Spira Records, an independent record label that pulsates to the rhythm of deep and hypnotic techno music. The name comes from the Latin verb "spīrāre", which means "to breathe," and is intended to be music that portrays connections between soul and brain.

The concept is rooted in the powerful metaphor of a deep breath—a moment of mindfulness, inspiration and transformation. We strive to evoke the sensation of a deep breath through our music—a moment of calmness amidst the chaos, a pause that allows us to connect with our inner selves. Our music encourages listeners to pause, immerse themselves, and connect with the reflective rhythms of the universe that we have created.
